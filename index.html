About

I am a Ph.D. student in Political Science at the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE) in Recife, Brazil. I received an M.A. in Political Science from UFPE in 2024 and a B.A. in Political Science from the same institution in 2022.

My research focuses on how international organizations process competing demands, allocate attention across issue areas, and adapt decision-making routines over time. I am particularly interested in the United Nations, global health governance, comparative politics, and how processes of democratization affect national and international institutions.
