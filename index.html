About

Political science research on international organizations and global governance.

I am a Ph.D. student in Political Science at the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE). My work focuses on international relations, international organizations, global health governance, the United Nations General Assembly, the United Nations Security Council, comparative politics, democratization, populism, and both quantitative and qualitative methods.

Current Focus

I study how global institutions process political agendas, with special attention to multilateral organizations, health governance, co-sponsorship, and the politics of institutional change.
